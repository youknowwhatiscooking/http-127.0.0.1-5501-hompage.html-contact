Company Overview

Custard Innovations Corporations is a team of dynamic and young individuals who aim to create value for their clients with their state-of-the-art technology and IT services. As a business process outsourcing company, Custard Innovations Corporations aims to provide their prowess and skills in crafting short-term and long-term solutions for all business issues. Custard Innovations Corporations is driven by excellence, integrity, professionalism, teamwork, flexibility and client-orientation. With its excellent support services and strong commitment to client relationships, Custard Innovations Corporations aims to establish itself as one of the best IT outsourcing companies in Philippines.

The core specialties of Custard Innovations Corporations include technical support, desktop publishing, back-office support, multimedia, web design and development and IT system development. Custard Innovations Corporations integrity is established by their conformity to moral and ethical codes of conduct. They are constantly striving to bring improvements in their expertise and skills for fostering organizational growth.

Custard Innovations Corporations believe in developing mutually beneficial relationships with clients and serving them with the technical support they need. Custard Innovations Corporations bring together the skills and know-how of professionals who are committed to client satisfaction at all levels of interaction. They are headquartered in General Santos City, Philippines.
